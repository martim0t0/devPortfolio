About me

Hi! I’m Marcin from the Chicago area, I have been here most of my life. I like the weather here, honestly!

Creative problem solving is what has ultimately led me to web development. This is where I get to utilize my favorite set of skills to build something that’s both functional and beautiful. I love to start with an empty canvas, make a plan for what we need, and figuring out how to make that happen. The accomplishment of a completed project is its own reward.

Outside of problem solving on the internet my hobbies are photography, creating digital art, aviation, gourmet cooking (seriously, It’s like a fancy restaurant here), and enjoying nature’s beauty in all weather except wind.
